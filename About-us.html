About us

Welcome to shri vishwakarma institute

Shri Vishwakarma Institute (SVI) and it is known as the best stenography institute in dera bassi. It is established in 1988 in dera bassi by Master. Prem Chand Kumar and he is the director of the the SVI located near telephone exchange, opp. Valmiki mandir, Tehsil Road ,Dera bassi, District. S.A.S Nagar Mohali , Punjab 140507.

The institute provides full certified education and after completing the course we also gives a government certified certificate to the student which should be very helpful for students future. Our institute also provides free books for the poor childrens and we provides educations from open school boards from poor peoples.
