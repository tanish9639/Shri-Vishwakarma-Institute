Main Courses

The goal of a basic computer course is to fulfill students with basic computer skills. Add on advanced courses may be taken to strengthen knowledge in an area. The defination below highlight the prospectus of some common basic computer courses.

Introduction to Computers

Students who learned very few with computers should learn the computer basics in this class. The achievements of this course is to show to people how important computers are in modern era and their value in our daily life. Main operations and functions of the computer will be covered. Topics includes the use of open software applications, the use of Internet, basic computer functions and the proper labelling of computer hardware components.

Keyboarding

Typing is a key feature of computer basics. Many job finders have lack of keyboarding skills. This course make students to learn the basics of touch and typing by using a computer program, such as Typing tutor for Windows. Students who already knows about touching and typing will use a typing software to improve their speed and accuracy of typing.

Microsoft Office

Knowledge of Microsoft Office is most important when searching for a new job. Many employers expect jobbers to have a basic knowledge of Microsoft Word, MS Excel and MS PowerPoint. This class typically covers the basics to how to set up a document in MS Word, and how to use a spreadsheet and create a presentation. This covers mid term of computer basics

Internet Navigation

Students also in this course will learn how to successfully navigate to the Internet. Some of the topics includes clouding computers, advanced search engines , travelling planning, social networking like facebook and instagram, blogging, bookmarking and how to get protection against various types of malfunctions, such as viruses and worms.

Course Delivery Information

Typically, a institute or college uses a combination of technologies and a learning system, such as wallboards, to deliver all content of courses .The role is to provide a real time learning surrounding that make a familiar manner between students and the professors.
