Introduction

Typing is the criteria of writing of input the text by pressing keys on a typewriter (used while we learn to type first time) , computer keyboard, cell phone, or calculator. It can be differenced from other means of text input, such as handwriting and recognition of speech . Text should be in a form of letters, numbers and symbols.

Touch typing in Dera Bassi

The main technique in keyboarding is contrasting to hunt and peck typing in which the typist has to keep his/ her eyes on the source of copy at all the times. Typing and touching also includes the use of the row method that are called as Home row, where typers keep their hand wrists up, rather than resting them on a desk or keyboard (which can cause carpal tunnel syndrome). To delay this type of typing, typists should sat up higher, leaning slightly forward from the waist, place their foots flat on the floor in besides of them with one foot coming in front of the other, and keeping their elbows nearest to their sides with forearms slants slightly upwards to the keyboard and fingers should be curved slightly and rest on the home row. Many of the touch typists also use keyboard shortcuts or hotkeys processing when typing on a computer. This process users to edit their documents without having to taking off their hands on keyboard for using a mouse. An good example of a keyboard shortcut is pressing the Ctrl key with plus the S key to save a document without having hands on the mouse, or the Ctrl key with the plus the Z key to undoing a mistake without using a mouse. Many of the experienced typists can get smell or feel sense whenever they had made an mistake and can hit the backspace key and make the correction in the words with no increase in time between keystrokes. Double fingered typing (Hunt and peck), also known as Eagle Finger. It is a common form of typing in which the typist press the each key individual. Instead of forgetting on the memorized position of keys, the sight of the typist find the key in the keyboard. Using of this method may also prevent the typist from being to see what has been typed without seeing away from the keys of keyboard. Although good accuracy and speed may be achieved by the typist,. There will be also a disadvantage that because of fewer fingers are used in eagle finger typing ,that makes a user to forced to going to a different key on keyboard.

Hybrid Typing In Derabassi

There are many individual typing styles in between learning-style "Eagle Fingered" and touch typing. For example, many "Eagle fingered" typists have the memorized keyboard layout and they are able to type while focused on their eye on the screen. Some people just use two fingers, while others use 6-3 fingers. Some use their fingers very softly, with the same finger they used to type the same characters every time, while others have their own way to type.
